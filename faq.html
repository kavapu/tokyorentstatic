Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about renting apartments in Tokyo

What documents do I need to rent an apartment?

You'll need a valid passport, visa (if applicable), proof of income or financial support, and a Japanese guarantor or guarantor company.

How long can I rent for?

We offer flexible rental periods from 1 month to 2 years. Short-term rentals (1-6 months) and long-term rentals (6+ months) are available.

Do you provide furnished apartments?

Yes! Most of our apartments come fully furnished with basic amenities including furniture, kitchen appliances, and internet connection.

What's included in the rent?

Rent typically includes basic utilities (water, gas, electricity) and internet. Additional services like cleaning can be arranged for an extra fee.

Can I rent without speaking Japanese?

Absolutely! Our staff speaks English and we provide full support in English throughout the rental process.

How far in advance should I apply?

We recommend applying at least 2-4 weeks before your desired move-in date to ensure smooth processing and availability.

What neighborhoods do you serve?

We have properties across Tokyo including popular areas like Shibuya, Shinjuku, Harajuku, Asakusa, and many more.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, typically 1-2 months rent as security deposit, which is refundable upon move-out if the apartment is in good condition.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact us directly.
