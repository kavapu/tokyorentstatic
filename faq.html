Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about renting flats in Tokyo

What documents do I need to rent an flat?

  • Valid passport and visa (if applicable)
  • Proof of income or financial support
  • Japanese guarantor or guarantor company

How long can I rent for?

  • Short-term: 1-6 months
  • Long-term: 6+ months
  • Maximum: 2 years

Do you provide furnished flats?

  • Fully furnished with furniture
  • Kitchen appliances included
  • Internet connection provided

What's included in the rent?

  • Basic utilities (water, gas, electricity)
  • Internet connection
  • Cleaning services available (extra fee)

Can I rent without speaking Japanese?

  • English-speaking staff
  • All documents in English
  • 24/7 English support available

What areas do you cover?

  • Central Tokyo (Shibuya, Shinjuku, Roppongi)
  • Popular expat areas
  • Near major train stations

How do I apply for a flat?

  • Fill out online application form
  • Submit required documents
  • We'll contact you within 24 hours

Is there a security deposit?

  • Amount: 1-2 months rent
  • Fully refundable
  • Returned if flat is in good condition

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact us directly.
