Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about renting flats in Tokyo. Everything you need to know about our services, application process, and rental policies.

What documents do I need to rent a flat?

  • Valid passport and visa (if applicable)
  • Proof of income or financial support
  • Japanese guarantor or guarantor company
  • Employment certificate or student ID
  • Previous rental history (if available)

How long can I rent for?

  • Short-term rentals: 1-6 months
  • Long-term rentals: 6+ months
  • Maximum rental period: 2 years
  • Flexible renewal options available
  • Early termination possible with notice

Do you provide furnished flats?

  • All flats come fully furnished
  • Complete kitchen appliances included
  • High-speed internet connection provided
  • Bedding and towels available
  • Cleaning supplies provided

What's included in the rent?

  • Basic utilities (water, gas, electricity)
  • High-speed internet connection
  • Building maintenance and security
  • Cleaning services (weekly)
  • 24/7 emergency support

Can I rent without speaking Japanese?

  • English-speaking staff available
  • All documents provided in English
  • Multilingual support team
  • Translation services included
  • Cultural orientation provided

What areas do you cover?

  • Central Tokyo (Shibuya, Shinjuku, Roppongi)
  • Popular expat neighborhoods
  • Areas near major train stations
  • University districts
  • Business and entertainment districts

How do I apply for a flat?

  • Fill out our online application form
  • Submit required documents
  • Schedule a viewing (optional)
  • We'll contact you within 24 hours
  • Sign lease agreement and pay deposit

Is there a security deposit?

  • Security deposit: 1-2 months rent
  • Fully refundable upon move-out
  • Returned if flat is in good condition
  • Deductions only for actual damages
  • Detailed inspection report provided

What are the payment methods?

  • Bank transfer (preferred)
  • Credit card payments accepted
  • International wire transfers
  • Monthly automatic payments available
  • Multiple currency options

What if I have problems with my flat?

  • 24/7 emergency hotline available
  • Maintenance team on standby
  • Online support ticket system
  • Regular property inspections
  • Quick response guarantee

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact us directly. Our team is here to help you with any questions or concerns.
